Regulating Gene Editing Technology: Critical Perspectives

Research

For this project I began with doing some research on controversial topics. I had recently watched a documentary on Netflix about gene editing (You can watch the preview here: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WIIVh7H6nvI), and I found the topic both fascinating and terrifying. Once I had my topic, I began doing research using credible sources like academic and government agency reports.

Design Decisions & Iterations

I made a loose argumentation structure (pictured here) and then filled in the blanks for each topic using the research I had gathered.

I learned a lot about the potential of gene editing while writing this essay (click on the link up top to have a read, I promise it's not too long!). Mainly though, I really enjoyed thinking about the ethical dilemmas involved and my responsibility as a designer. I have to make sure that I thoroughly research a topic to understand the pros and cons as well as the grey area.
