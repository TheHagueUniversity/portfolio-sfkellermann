About Me

Hi, I'm Sara.

I am half Dutch and half American, born in Germany, but raised mainly in the US. By the time I was 18, I had lived in 7 cities in 3 countries spanning 2 continents. My endless curiosity led to me add several more cities to that list, including Boston, Miami, New York and finally The Hague. Needless to say, I love to travel!

So, why UX?

Since moving to The Hague, I have been working in digital fraud prevention. In this job, I resolve user issues by working with back office and front office systems and communicating directly with users. As the potential impacts of the interactions between front and backoffice systems on the end users became clearer to me, I realized that I have a lot of ideas on how to improve them! That's when I discovered UX.
